Robotics

Selected robotics work: mechanisms, control, and perception demos.

This project shows how the 7-DoF DE NIRO robot can manipulate and interact with objects using three control modes. With position control, we computed end-effector orientations (Euler â†’ quaternion) and executed smooth pick-and-place and two-arm hand-over while avoiding collisions. With velocity control, Jacobians and the pseudo-inverse produced joint speeds and used null-space redundancy for obstacle avoidance. With torque/force controland a PD scheme, the robot demolished a wall and swept a table, tuning for stable, safe contact.
